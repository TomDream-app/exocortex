# Processes

The exocortex operates through three fundamental flows.

capture → mapping → consolidation

---

## Capture

Material enters the system as raw elements.

Examples:

- notes
- images
- conversations
- transcripts
- links

At this stage no strong interpretation is imposed.

The goal is to accumulate material.

---

## Mapping

Captured material is analyzed to reveal potential relations.

Possible mechanisms:

- semantic similarity
- embeddings
- clustering
- conceptual proximity

This stage reveals patterns without fixing them.

---

## Consolidation

When a pattern becomes meaningful, it can be stabilized.

Consolidation produces structures such as:

- concepts
- models
- frameworks

The decision to consolidate always remains human.
