# Walking

## Status

`walking` is the first pilot domain of Exocortex.

It is not introduced as an application, and not as a core primitive.
It is introduced as a structured source field.

## Why this domain exists

The walking domain emerged from a broader reflection on:
- movement,
- static and dynamic regimes,
- rhythm,
- attention,
- writing,
- morphology,
- and the relation between locomotion and conscious structuration.

This material has enough density to justify a dedicated domain.

## Function inside Exocortex

The walking domain serves three roles:

1. A field of inquiry in its own right
2. A possible editorial branch (including book-form output)
3. A pilot terrain for future transformation into a navigable cognitive landscape

## Main axes

The domain may include:
- walking as experience
- walking and consciousness
- walking and rhythm
- walking and morphology
- walking and writing
- locomotor cycles
- movement and memory
- spatial attention
- dynamic vs static organization

## Relation to the broader project

The walking domain is part of Exocortex, but it is not reducible to the software architecture alone.

It may develop simultaneously as:
- research material,
- structured notes,
- corpus,
- conceptual mapping,
- and editorial output.

## Internal structure

Suggested internal folders:
- `notes/`
- `corpus/`
- `book/`

## Principle

Walking is treated here as a field capable of generating structure, not only as a topic.
